Delivery of Music to All

Our project attempted to make the connection between music
and those who can’t listen to music
and its messages due to their physical disabilities.

It is specifically for those who might want to deliver a song’s message to someone
who can not hear- through sign language.
Lyrics of a song is translated into sign language with pictures to follow.
This way you can now “sing” to someone who can not hear singing.
